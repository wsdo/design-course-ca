About our Livestreams

Meng Li

Meng Li is the author of Design+Code. Meng started off his career as a self-taught designer from Montreal and eventually traveled around the world for 2 years as his US VISA was denied. During his travels, he wrote a book which now has 35,000 readers.

UI Design Livestreams

This is a compilation of the UI live streams hosted by Meng. Over there he talks and teaches how to use design systems, typography, navigation, iOS 14 Design, prototyping, animation and Developer Handoff.

In this Livestream, Meng talks about his design process and the things that inspire him. He shows some tips and tricks on creating beautiful UI, and he shares some websites where you can grab inspiration and some other tools.
